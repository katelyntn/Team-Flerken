Current Offerings

Hansen Vineyard Pinot Noir



This elegant wine overflows with bright blueberry with hints of toasted oak aromas that adds complexity and interest. The silky-smooth and strong texture will keep you wanting more.


Leras Vineyard Pinot Noir



This blend of Pinot Noir is an elegant wine with aromas of black cherry, blackberry accented by notes of vanilla and chocolate. Smooth, sleek and velvety.

Bright fruit aromas are met with delicate flavors of strawberry and soft lemon-lime. A soft yet vibrant texture and a crisp but lingering finish make this a perfect match for a variety of dishes to pair with.


Sonoma Coma Vineyard Pinot Noir



Vibrant ruby color of this Pinot Noir showcases intense flavors of wild blueberries, red raspberry, and back notes of pomegranate, vanilla, and herbal hints.
